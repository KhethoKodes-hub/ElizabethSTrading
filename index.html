Elizabeth Segametse Brits Trading is a proudly Black Empowered company based in Postmasburg, delivering culinary excellence for every occasion — from intimate boardroom meetings to large-scale government galas.

Our founder, Mrs. Elizabeth Segametse Brits, fuses 10+ years of financial sector experience with deep hospitality expertise. We serve government departments, local mines in the Postmasburg district, corporates, and private clients with unmatched passion and punctuality.
