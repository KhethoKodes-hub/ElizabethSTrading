BEE & Impact

Black Economic
Empowerment & Community

As a fully BEE Level 1 compliant enterprise, our operational guidelines mirror the principles of the Black Economic Empowerment Act. We are proud to be a catalyst for local economic development — hiring from previously disadvantaged backgrounds, sourcing local produce, and uplifting Postmasburg's entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Our fully-equipped kitchen in Postmasburg uses fresh vegetables, quality meats, and dairy, preparing most dishes from scratch under strict chef-led sanitation standards. We cater for events from 10 to 200+ guests, delivering to offices, home venues, or any external location across the Northern Cape.
